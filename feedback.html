THE IT WORLD
I have collected three feedbacks from my classmates and here are their words:

“I think your idea is really interesting and might be helpful to many people. However, I am concerned about some people will be depend too much on the app and close their door with the real world. Will talking to the app reduce their depression or their interaction with other people? It can make them feel quite relax at first since they can release their depression to an online friend, however, that will make them less want to talk to others. This might not happen all the time but I think there is a high possibility of this.” – Minh Trang



“The project idea about creating an app called "Best Friend" is very interesting. It can be used for some mental treatment methods for depressed or unmotivated people by creating a 3D human character that can listen to user's story and give them some advice. In order to improve this project idea, the voice recording needs to be more specific (whose voice will the app will use? What voice tone that the developer will choose? etc….). But overall, the idea to create an 3D friend is very interesting.” – Nguyễn Huy



“I love Linh's idea of getting as authentic as possible virtual therapy. However, she can integrate a specific disorder and how that virtual friend can help their customers improve their current conditions in a specific way. For example, in an eating disorder, the friend will have the eating tracking of the frequencies and ingredients that their customers consume and give them personalized pieces of advice, recommended substitutions. Though her course objective can be inferred throughout the description's structure, it is better to state headings in bold.” – Anh Thư
