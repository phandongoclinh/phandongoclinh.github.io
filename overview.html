Overview

Are you scared of events full of strangers? Are you socially awkward and dislike making conversations? Do you have mysophobia and haphephobia but still want to enjoy vacations with a virtual tour guy? No need to worry, our Best Friend app will make it easier for you. Our purpose is to help people who are suffering with anti social related disorder to have a healthier, social-friendlier life. All you need is a smartphone, download our app and we will lead you as well as give you guides in all types of social gatherings.

Motivation

To one who experiences fears of human contact, for example haphephobia or social anxiety disorder, they are limited in participating in everyday activities. According to Healthline, ‘Haphephobia is more than just cringing inwardly when someone gives you an unwanted hug or invades your personal space on the subway. Instead, it’s an often-paralyzing fear that can have a devastating effect on your life if untreated. This feeling of paralysis is what separates someone who is merely uncomfortable with touch from someone who has a true phobia.’ (‘What Is Haphephobia and How Can You Manage Fear of Touch?’ 2017). With this application, everyone can have a virtual friend who will tell them how to react, what to say or where to go in just one touch. It is a good, overall resolutions to solve people with social problems, specially during the pandemic like this where we would like to keep our distance from others.
